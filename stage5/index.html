About Us

Founded in 2020, Petal & Bloom has been serving the Istanbul community with passion, creativity, and an unwavering love for flowers. What started as a small corner shop near Yıldız Teknik Üniversitesi's Davutpaşa Campus has grown into one of the neighborhood's most beloved floral destinations.

Our team of expert florists brings together years of experience in both traditional Turkish floral art and contemporary European design. We source our flowers fresh daily from local growers and the renowned Istanbul flower market, ensuring every arrangement is vibrant, fragrant, and long-lasting.

At Petal & Bloom, we believe that flowers are more than decoration — they are a language. A single rose can say "I love you." A bright sunflower can say "I'm thinking of you." We are here to help you find the right words, wrapped in petals.
